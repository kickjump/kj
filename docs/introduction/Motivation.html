1.1. Motivation

Motivation

Technology is moving fast. And the pace is getting quicker by the year. It's almost makes one crazy thinking about the metling po. As developers there are always ten new things we'd love to try out, yet the day doesn't have enough time to even consider sinking our teeth into potentially fulfilling side projects.

While hard to quantify, it feels like the JavaScript community is moving at a break neck speed. As more developers come on board, and as JavaScript has become a truly multiplatform experience, on a daily basis there brand new frameworks, build tools, best practises and ways of working. It's become impossible to keep up.

kj can change this explosive cluster of shit that we're cauught in. In fact we think it's pretty great. What kj tries to do is provide an insight into the latest and greatest tools for delivering multiplatform experiences to your users.

Whether you're looking to deploy on the web only, or across, mobile, tablets and desktop, this might be the tool for you. At for two weeks, until something better comes along.
